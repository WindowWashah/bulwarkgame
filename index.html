Siege of the Keep

Build towers on the dark tiles to stop the horde before it reaches your keep. Earn gold for kills, then click any tower to invest in one of its two upgrade paths.

Counters matter: Wraiths fly (only Tesla, Arcane, Ballista, Tempest & Prism hit air) · armored foes shrug off chip damage, so use heavy hits, poison or fire · Acolytes heal the horde · Hollows split when slain.
Status combos: a heavy hit on a slowed foe Shatters for +50% · a foe both burning & poisoned Detonates in a blast (mix Pyre + Venom) · Tesla bolts Conduct through chilled foes for +30% and arc farther (pair with Frost).
Link towers: place two adjacent (even diagonally) for +6% fire rate each (max +24%); matching pairs grant named bonuses — Archer+Cannon (Volley Line), Frost+Venom (Toxic Chill), Tesla+Prism (Resonance), Cannon+Pyre (Firestorm Battery), Ballista+Arcane (Siege Focus).
